Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 8 slices

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Beat in the eggs and milk.
  4. Pour into a baking dish and bake for 25 minutes.
